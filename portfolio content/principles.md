# Principles

These are reminders that take me closer to who I am. I need them time to time. Carved out based on experiences lived through.
- Inspired from [nabeelqu's principles note](https://nabeelqu.co/principles)
- Feel free to steal what you like

———

1. **Be you. Inside out. Authenticity brings you peace.**
   - No-one can compete with you on being you (via naval)

2. **Have agency over your agency.**

3. **Separation of task.** Take ownership and focus only on your task.

4. **You can only control your actions + how you respond.**

5. **You always have a choice on how you respond.**

6. **Anger is hot coal you hold in your hands waiting to throw at someone else** (via naval - via buddhist philosophy)

7. **Look at the bigger picture every once in a while - it's beautiful.**

8. **Have courage to be disliked** - it's okay, it frees you. (via Alfred Adler)

9. **Self worth is an abstraction, has no meaning.** Tying achievement to your worth is a self defeating concept - focus on what you find interesting, satisfying - keep having fun.

10. **Show up and let the magic happen.**

11. **Operate from your strengths.**

12. **Decision quality >>> decision speed.**

13. **Slow is smooth, smooth is fast.**

14. **Outcomes are lagging indicators, focus on your inputs** - strawberry farmer.
    - Don't judge each day by harvest you reap, but the seeds you plant.
    - Don't chase butterflies, build garden, let butterflies come and chill.

15. **There are more parasites than parasitical organism, optimise for symbiotes.**

16. **Keep going.**

17. **Compassion. Kindness** - towards self and people around.

18. **Abundance. Positive sum games.** [There's no finite wealth.](https://paulgraham.com/wealth.html) Make something people want.
