## 02 · 78Health

**Remote patient monitoring · [Naren Nachiappan](https://www.linkedin.com/in/naren-nachiappan-036387/), 4× founder · Pre-PMF · California + Bengaluru**

Physician-led startup operating across the US and India. Mapped their $6M revenue path, found the missing funnel denominators, and stress-tested the fundraise.

**What I did**

- Identified the core constraint: clinician time
  - One physician capped at ~20 patients/day
  - 78Health's model unlocks 1,500+ patients per physician
- Built the revenue math: $6M run rate = 0.2 take rate × $250k/physician × 120 physicians
- Mapped the full funnel — physician signing to first live patient
  - Flagged the missing denominators their model wasn't tracking: active patients per physician, CAC, retention speed, activation rate
- Ran a pre-mortem on the fundraise and two-market risk (US + India bandwidth split)
- Asked the hard questions: EMR integration risk, platform risk from Epic/Cerner, why two markets simultaneously

**What happened**

- Got to offer discussion — equity, comp, onboarding — before fundraise timing created uncertainty
